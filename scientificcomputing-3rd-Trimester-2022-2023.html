Course Overview

Main concepts and ideas of scientiÔ¨Åc computing will be presented in this course. The course consists of two parts, a theoretical part and a practical part. The theoretical part presents mathematical methods and algorithms concerning elementary problems in numerical analysis. The practical part helps the students to implement mathematical methods and algorithms. We will use MATLAB, which is a programming language and a computing environment, for the practical part.
